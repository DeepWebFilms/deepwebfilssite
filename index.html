Stephen Gaffney is an Irish director and writer

Red Room. Bully. Class A.

Stephen Gaffney is an Irish filmmaker known for his raw, unapologetic style and fearless exploration of dark subject matter. From micro-budget horror beginnings to working on Netflix productions, his voice remains uniquely personal and provocative. His credits include Red Room, Bully, and Class A, establishing him as one of the boldest independent voices in Irish cinema.
