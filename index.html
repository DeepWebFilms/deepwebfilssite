Deep Web Films Distribution

We are a boutique label and treat our clients as friends. Every project we take on is handled with genuine care, attention, and mutual respect.

Founded by Stephen Gaffney, Deep Web Films Distribution brings fearless, uncompromising horror to international audiences. Our catalogue represents the boldest voices in independent cinema.

We started with a micro-budget feature — Bully — and grew into working on major productions for platforms like Netflix. That journey fuels our mission: to help emerging filmmakers break through and get noticed.

Your film means as much to us as it does to you. As a filmmaker myself, I understand the blood, sweat, and tears that go into making a film. With Deep Web Films Distribution, we’ll work with you to get your story out there — and ensure it reaches the audiences it deserves.
